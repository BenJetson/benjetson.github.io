Comment Policy

Certain pages on this site have a section where users are allowed to share comments about the page content with other site users. These comment sections are powered by Disqus.

Guidelines

When writing comments or responding to others, please observe the guidelines below. These guidelines will be strictly enforced by moderators.

  • Relevant and respectful comments are welcome.
  • Don't post anything here that you wouldn't say in person.
  • Off-topic or disrespectful comments will cause your account to be banned.
  • All comments require approval.

Disclaimer: Commenting on this site is a privilege, not a right. Accounts may be banned or individual comments deleted at the discretion of a moderator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Comments on this site require approval by a moderator prior to being displayed to the public.

Comments are manually reviewed by both humans and robots prior to publication. While the robots are fairly quick at making a decision, your comment will have to wait for a human moderator to press the approve button prior to publication. As a result, comments may stay in the pending state for an extended period.

You can use the contact page to get in touch with a moderator. Please link to the comment thread in question in the body of your message.

When a discussion board is locked, no further comments or replies may be posted.

Discussion boards for blog posts will lock automatically when a certain amount of time has passed since the post was published.

Other pages may have their comments locked at the discretion of a moderator.
